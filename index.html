Learning process
Preprocessing and analysis of data in real life tasks take up to 90% of the time solution of problems.
Three Pillars of Learning
1.Data- examples of solutions and everything that can help in the learning process. Data is collected over the years and combined into huge arrays - datasets.
2.Features - they are properties or characteristics. This is what the machine should pay attention to in the learning process.
3.Algorithms - way of solving a problem. There can be many of them for the same task, and it is important to choose the most accurate and efficient one.
Main types of machine learning
Ð¡lassical training - These are the simplest algorithms that solve formal problems.
Reinforcement learning - This is a more complex type of training, where you need to not only analyze data, but act independently in a real environment.
Ensembles - These are groups of algorithms that use several machine learning methods at once and correct each other's errors.
Neural networks and deep learning - Neural networks simulate the work of the human brain, which consists of neurons constantly forming new connections with each other.
